Everything you need to launch & scale experiences

Operate like a modern adventure hub with a unified dashboard, automated communications, and tools that empower hosts and members alike.

  • Listings: Rich media gallery, kit lists, requirements, and add-on bundles.
  • Bookings: Tiered pricing, membership gating, flexible payment windows.
  • Operations: Guide rosters, risk management workflows, and incident logs.
  • Engagement: Resend-powered drip campaigns, drop alerts, and post-trip follow-up.

How the marketplace flows

  1. Discover: Filter by activity, skill level, accessibility, and host rating.
  2. Reserve: Tap to book with instant confirmation or join the waitlist.
  3. Equip: Add rentals, shuttles, or meal kits in one checkout.
  4. Adventure: Receive guide briefs, route maps, and real-time notifications.
  5. Re-live: Share recaps, earn credits, and review your host.
